About the Founder/CEO - Yahaya Abubakar

Yahaya Abubakar is the visionary Founder and and current CEO of Bioinformatics and Data Science Innovative Centre (BioDIC). He graduated with First Class Honours in Biochemistry from Nasarawa State University, Keffi, where he demonstrated exceptional academic prowess and a deep passion for life sciences. Yahaya's commitment to excellence and innovation has been the driving force behind his remarkable achievements in the field. Currently pursuing his M.Sc. in Biochemistry, Yahaya specializes in Bioinformatics, Drug Discovery, and Infectious Disease Modeling. His advanced studies have equipped him with a comprehensive understanding of the intersection between biology and technology, enabling him to pioneer cutting-edge research in these critical areas. With a strong foundation in biochemistry and a forward-thinking approach, Yahaya is dedicated to integrating emerging technologies into life sciences to address some of the most pressing challenges in health and medicine. Yahaya's vision for BioDIC stems from his passion for bridging the gap between academic knowledge and industry demands. He recognizes the importance of equipping life sciences graduates with practical, hands-on experience in bioinformatics and data science, preparing them for the rapidly evolving biotech industry. Under his leadership, BioDIC is not only a training hub but also a center for groundbreaking research in genomics, proteomics, drug discovery, and infectious disease modeling. Driven by his belief in the transformative power of technology, Yahaya Abubakar is committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and excellence at BioDIC. His leadership ensures that the center remains at the forefront of scientific discovery, making significant contributions to both academia and industry.
